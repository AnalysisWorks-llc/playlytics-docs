# Getting Started with Voice Logging

## Requirements

Voice logging requires a paid subscription (Player, Coach, or Organization). Free accounts do not have access to voice logging.

**Supported browsers:**
- Android: Chrome (recommended)
- iOS: Safari
- Desktop: Chrome or Edge

Firefox has limited voice logging support and is not recommended.

## First Time Setup

### Android

1. Install Playlytics to your home screen via Chrome
2. Go to **Settings → General Management → Keyboard list and default**
3. Set **Google Voice Typing** as your default input method
4. Return to Playlytics — the microphone is now always one tap away in the keyboard

### iOS

No extra setup required. Safari handles voice input natively.

### Desktop

No setup required. Click the microphone button and speak.

## Accessing Voice Logging During a Game

1. Start a game session from the dashboard
2. From the **Game Hub** tap **Voice Log**
3. The voice logging interface opens

On mobile the keyboard appears automatically. Tap the microphone icon on the keyboard, speak your play description, then tap the arrow/send button.

On desktop tap the microphone button, speak, and the recording stops automatically.

## Review and Confirm

After speaking a **confirmation card** appears showing:
- The logger type the AI identified (Pitching, Hitting, Fielding, or Baserunning)
- The player matched from your roster
- The specific fields parsed from your description
- Zone pickers to visually confirm or correct location data

Tap **Confirm and Save** to log the play.
Tap **Edit** to open the manual logger with all fields pre-filled.
Tap **Try Again** to re-speak the play.
