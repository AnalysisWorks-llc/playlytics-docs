# Roster Management

## Overview

Your roster is the foundation of Playlytics. Every stat, analytics chart, and report card is tied to a player on your roster. Keeping your roster accurate and up to date ensures your analytics are meaningful.

## Adding Players

1. Navigate to **Roster** from the bottom navigation
2. Tap **+ Add Player**
3. Enter:
   - First name and last name
   - Jersey number
   - Primary position(s)
   - Secondary position(s) — used by the defensive lineup builder
4. Optionally add:
   - Date of birth
   - Parent name and contact information
   - Notes
5. Tap **Save**

## Editing Player Information

Tap any player card on the Roster screen to open their profile. Tap **Edit** to modify any field.

Jersey numbers and positions can be updated at any time without affecting historical data.

## Archiving Players

When a player leaves the team tap **Archive** on their profile rather than deleting them. Archived players:
- Are hidden from the active roster
- Retain all their historical data
- Can be restored if they return to the team
- Do not count against your player limit

## Player Profiles

Each player profile shows:
- Basic information and contact details
- Season stat summary
- Recent performance trend
- Active training plans
- Mechanics assessment history
- Coach notes

Tap **View Full Analytics** from the player profile to access the complete analytics dashboard for that player.

## Roster for Analytics

The analytics engine uses your roster to:
- Match voice-logged plays to the correct player
- Generate player-specific training suggestions
- Build lineup recommendations based on position data
- Create player report cards

Keep player positions accurate for the best lineup builder recommendations.

## Sharing Roster Access

Players and parents added to your team can view the roster. Parents see only their own child's detailed analytics — they see aggregate team stats for other players but not individual detail.
