# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**Does Playlytics work for both baseball and softball?**
Yes. When you create a team you select Baseball or Softball. The app adjusts field diagrams, pitch types, mechanics assessments, and analytics thresholds automatically. A single subscription covers teams of both sports.

**Can I use Playlytics without a paid subscription?**
Free accounts can create a team, add players, and enter data using all loggers. Analytics, training plans, report cards, and lineup builders require a paid subscription. This lets you build up a season of data before subscribing.

**Can multiple people log data at the same time?**
Yes. All team members with Scorekeeper role or higher can log data simultaneously. Changes are synced in real time.

**How do I switch between teams?**
Use the team selector in the app header or on the dashboard. The active team controls which roster, analytics, and settings are shown throughout the app.

## Data Entry

**What happens if I make a mistake logging data?**
Records can be freely edited or deleted within 2 hours of entry. After 2 hours a correction reason is required and the edit is logged for transparency. Head coaches can correct any record at any time.

**Can I log data from video after a game?**
Yes. Start a game session and select **Unscored Past Games** — any scheduled game without stats appears here. Log stats normally from video.

**Does voice logging work in Spanish?**
Spanish language support is coming soon.

## Analytics

**Why is my ERA showing ERA (6-inn) or ERA (7-inn)?**
Playlytics uses age-appropriate innings for ERA calculation. 8U–12U teams use 6-inning ERA and 13U and above use 7-inning ERA. See [Understanding ERA](/analytics/era-explained) for details.

**How much data do I need before analytics are meaningful?**
As a general guide — batting average after 20+ at-bats, ERA after 10+ innings, fielding percentage after 20+ chances, mechanics after 5+ sessions.

**Can parents see their child's analytics?**
Yes. Parent role accounts have read access to all team analytics. Players can see their own stats highlighted in team leaderboards.

## Subscriptions

**Can I change my plan?**
Yes. Go to Settings → Manage Subscription to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel through the Stripe Customer Portal. Changes take effect at the next billing period.

**What happens when my free trial ends?**
You retain access to data entry. Analytics, training plans, report cards, and lineup builders are paused until you subscribe. Your data is never deleted.

**Can I get a refund?**
Contact support at support@analysisworks-llc.com within 7 days of a charge for refund consideration.

## Technical

**Does Playlytics work offline?**
The app loads from cache when offline. Data entry requires an internet connection to save. Analytics load from cache if previously viewed.

**Which browsers are supported?**
Chrome and Safari are fully supported. Firefox has limited voice logging support. Edge works correctly.

**Voice logging does not work on my device — what should I do?**
Voice logging requires Chrome on Android or Safari on iOS. On Android, setting Google Voice Typing as your default keyboard improves reliability. See [Mobile Voice Logging](/voice-logging/mobile) for setup instructions.
