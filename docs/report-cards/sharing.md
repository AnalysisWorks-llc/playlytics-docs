# Sharing Report Cards with Families

## Generating a Share Link

1. Generate the report card (see [Generating Report Cards](/report-cards/generating))
2. In the preview tap **Share**
3. A shareable link is generated automatically
4. Send via text, email, or any messaging app using the native share sheet

## What the Family Sees

The share link opens a read-only version of the report card in any browser — no Playlytics account required. The family sees:
- The same report card layout you see in preview
- All charts, stats, and coach comments
- Team branding (team name and sport)

They cannot edit anything, see other players, or access any other part of Playlytics.

## Link Expiration

Share links expire after 30 days. After expiration the link shows a "Report card no longer available" message. Generate a new report card and share a fresh link if families need access after expiration.

## Sharing Recommendations

**End of season** — generate report cards for the full season and share with all families as a season summary.

**Mid-season check-in** — share after a significant stretch of games (10+ games) when analytics are meaningful.

**After a mechanics session** — share immediately after an Individual Practice session where you conducted assessments. The data is fresh and specific.

**Tournament weekend** — share after a tournament where a player had a significant performance (good or bad) to provide context.

## Privacy

Report card links are not indexed by search engines and are not discoverable without the link. However treat them as semi-public — do not share links containing sensitive personal information beyond what is in the report card (player name, jersey number, stats).
