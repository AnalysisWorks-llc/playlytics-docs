## Player Plan

Designed for parents tracking their own child's development. Up to 5 player profiles with full analytics, training suggestions, and report cards. No team features — if your coach uses Playlytics their team subscription covers team-level access.

## Coach Plan

The most popular plan for travel and recreational team coaches. One team with unlimited players, full analytics, lineup builders, and multi-user access so assistant coaches and scorekeeping parents can contribute.

## Organization Plan

For club directors and organizations managing multiple teams. Up to 5 teams under one subscription with cross-team analytics and organization-level dashboard.

## Managing Your Subscription

Access subscription management through **Settings → Manage Subscription**. You can upgrade, downgrade, update your payment method, or cancel at any time through the Stripe Customer Portal.
